I created an HTML/CSS webpage for my creative writing project to highlight my identity. I am attending Cape Fear Community College for an Associates in Science and planning to transfer to North Carolina State University for a Bachelor's in Computer Science starting my Junior year. Software engineering has been an interest of mine, as the career runs in my family. I base myself on wanting to accomplish a certain lifestyle and career, taking any opportunity to highlight my knowledge and skills. I wanted to use my skillset in web development to highlight my interests, hobbies, and identity for my project. This website serves as both my final project for my Creative Writing project and for my personal portfolio, as I plan to expand my knowledge and work to pursue a career in software engineering and web development.
